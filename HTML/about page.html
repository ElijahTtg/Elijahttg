About me

Hi! I'm Elijah Tan, a Singapore Polytechnic student studying in Diploma in architecture. I am excited to embark on a career as an Architect because I am interested in exploring different ways in designing residential buildings that are both sustainable and functional.

In my time in Singapore Polytechnic, I participated in various projects and internships which have allowed me to showcase my strengths and skills in  presentation and layout using softwares such as Microsoft PowerPoint and Photoshop. I was also able to showcase my skills in other softwares such as AutoCAD and Sketchup. I hope to be able to work with other Architects and achieve career success by gaining the necessary experience in leadership and presentation skills through multiple projects.
